Kiki The Cats Favorite Things

Boxes

After much research. Top scientists have discovered that Kiki's favorite boxes are those that originally caried expensive merchandise.

With an even greater preference for the box when the expensive merchandise was purchased specifically for him to play with.

He will show complete apathy for the expensive item and put all his focus on the box.

Feather Wands

His nickname is "Murder Mittens". His nickname becomes immediately obvious when a feather wand is nearby.

He enters a state many would refer to as Murder Mode where he will stalk and pounce on the feather wand.

Many studies have been done and 9/10 scientists agree that it is "adorable". The other 1/10 were self described dog people.

Fancy Bandana Collars

It's unclear whether or not he actually likes the fancy bandana collars. If anything he probably just tolerates them.

Most people would agree though. He looks really good while wearing them.
